By:sai krishna

Why Healthy Eating Matters

Eating a healthy diet is essential for maintaining good health and well-being. It can help prevent a range of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. A balanced diet provides the body with essential nutrients needed to function correctly.

For more information on balanced diets, visit the World Health Organization website.

Nutrients Your Body Needs

A variety of nutrients are required to keep our bodies healthy. These include:

  • Proteins
  • Carbohydrates
  • Fats
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Water
